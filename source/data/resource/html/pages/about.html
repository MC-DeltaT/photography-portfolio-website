About

Reece Jones is a photographer based in North Sydney, Australia. Starting out in 2023, his journey with photography has always been one of experimentating and learning. Reece works with a variety of photography styles and subjects, with his current passions being people, nature, and abstract.


{# TODO: photo of me alongside #}

"My photography began from zero as a hobby, and it's evolved from there. It was never about being the best photographer ever, more like being a better photographer than myself a week ago. Much of what I've learnt has been self-taught - simply playing around, idealising a particular style ever now and then, and wondering what could be next.
The best part of this craft is it's basically only limited by what you and those around you can imagine. I love bringing a creative vision to life - bringing a part of our internal experience into reality. Seeing the smile on someone's face when your collaboration has brought about a way of seeing the world they never expected, that's priceless."
